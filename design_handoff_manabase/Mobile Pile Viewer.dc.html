Modals, built for the thumb.

Desktop moves cards by hovering and pressing H · D · S · T · Y. Touch has no hover and no keyboard — so every concept below turns that hidden shortcut layer into a visible, tappable destination surface, and makes selection batch-capable. Four mobile concepts, then how the chrome flexes for Scry & Discard, then two desktop toolbar passes.
