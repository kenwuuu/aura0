The interface for a faster game.

Menus float on a raised surface with a hairline outline and a deep shadow — the one place shadow is allowed, because the sheet leaves the plane. The active row is marked with a 2px accent rail and a faint purple wash, never a full fill.

Rest is a bare 1px frame. Hover lifts and blooms. Tapped rotates 20° with no glow — spent, quiet. Targeted pulses a continuous purple aura so the play reads instantly across the table. Every transition is spring-eased and under 200ms.

✦ PRISMATIC FOIL

The holographic layer blends with color-dodge, so it brightens light areas of the art and barely touches shadows — exactly how a real metallic surface throws light. A specular band sweeps on top, and a rainbow filament traces the edge. Works over any art, no per-card masking.
